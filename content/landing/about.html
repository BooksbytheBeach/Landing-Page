What we do

This isn't a book club.
It's a thinking gym.

Once a month, a small group of working professionals reads the same book — then sits down together to talk about it. In English. Without a script. You defend ideas you're not entirely sure you believe. You articulate things you've only half-thought through. You leave a little sharper than you arrived.

We keep the group small on purpose. Everyone is vetted. Everyone is here to grow. That's what makes the conversations worth showing up for.

People who challenge you
Every member is working, ambitious, and serious about growth. You won't be the smartest person in the room — and that's the point.
Real speaking practice
Every session includes impromptu presentations. No slides. No prep. Just you, your ideas, and a room full of people who want to hear them.
One book. Many perspectives.
Our members come from different industries, countries, and walks of life. The same book can look very different depending on who's reading it.
