How it works

Three steps
to grow with us

01
Follow us on Instagram
All session announcements, themes, and updates live on @booksbythebeachkr — that's where everything happens.
02
Meet the leaders
A 1:1 deep dive chat with the leaders so that we get to know you and your expectations for our circle better! Communication via video call or messaging will take place depending on the schedule.
03
Get onboard
Once you have signed up to become a member, a group orientation will take place. This is your moment to chat and connect with members of our reading society.
