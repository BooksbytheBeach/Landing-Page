How it works

Three steps
to get in

01
Follow us on Instagram
@booksbythebeachkr is where we post session themes, reading picks, and community updates. Start there to get a feel for who we are.
02
We get to know you
Before anything else, we sit down — via video call or DM — for a quick conversation. We want to understand where you are and what you're looking for. No pressure, no agenda. Just an honest chat.
03
You meet the circle
If it's a good fit, you'll join a group orientation before your first session. It's warm, informal, and a lot less scary than it sounds.
