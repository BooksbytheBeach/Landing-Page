Reels format

Two reel modes

Mode 1
Storytelling reels

Elaina tells a personal story — her own journey, community moments, or a member's experience. Emotion first, insight second. Hook in first 2 seconds.

Example openers

"I used to go blank every time someone asked me to speak in English..."
"The first time I ran Real Talk, only 4 people showed up..."
"A member told me something last week that I haven't stopped thinking about..."

Mode 2
Recommendation reels

Elaina recommends something — a book, habit, mindset shift, concept from a session. Structured, clear, confident. Authority without arrogance.

Example openers

"If you're a professional who reads zero books a year, this is for you..."
"Three books that will change how you think — not just how you speak..."
"The one habit that separates confident English speakers from everyone else..."
