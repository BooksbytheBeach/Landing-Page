Who is Elaina
Name & Role
Elaina — Founder and Head Lead of Books by the Beach (BBB) and Fluency Mastermind. Based in Seoul & Busan, Korea.
Her story
Elaina built BBB from scratch — Korea's only all-English reading society. She ran the first session with just a handful of people and turned it into a thriving community of professionals across Seoul and Busan. She's lived the journey from anxious, insecure, and highly self-conscious to grounded, controlled, and deeply purposeful — and that transformation shapes everything she creates.
Her mission
Helping professionals think sharp and speak clear through the power of critical reading. She believes real English fluency comes from authentic engagement — not study alone.
Her values
#Courage #Challenge #Curiosity Growth mindset Active listening No judgment Community over performance
Voice in one line
"Bold, warm, intellectually direct. Speaks like a trusted mentor who's been through it — not a polished brand."
What is BBB
Full name
Books by the Beach — Korea's all-English Reading Society. Active in Seoul and Busan.
What it is
A community of driven professionals who meet regularly to read, discuss, and grow — entirely in English. Not a casual book club. A structured, high-discourse environment where members sharpen their critical thinking, presentation skills, and leadership through deep reading.
What makes it different
BBB is built on the belief that real English fluency is earned through real conversations about real ideas — not through textbooks. Every session is in English. Every member is pushed to speak, share, and lead.
Event formats
Real Talk workshops BYOB reading nights PPT Nights Panel discussions Corporate workshops Partnership events
Brand handle
@booksbythebeachkr — Instagram, Threads, LinkedIn
Aesthetic in one line
"Literary magazine meets warm gathering. Warm linen tones, editorial serif type, real community photography."
Who BBB is for

Target audience

The ambitious professional
Proficient in English, working in Korea. Wants to think and communicate at a higher level — not just pass exams. Has studied abroad or works in international environments.
  • Wants to lose the psychological barrier when speaking English
  • Wants peers who challenge them intellectually
  • Wants to lead better, present better, think faster
The growth-oriented reader
Already reads — or wants to. Believes books are the fastest way to grow. Wants a community that holds them accountable and helps them apply what they read.
  • Wants deep conversations, not surface-level chat
  • Wants book recs that actually change how they think
  • Values consistent showing up over perfection
The expat / multicultural professional
Living in Korea, often isolated in finding community that matches their intellectual level. BBB is one of the rare spaces where they genuinely belong.
  • Wants authentic connection across backgrounds
  • Wants to feel at home in an international environment
  • Values warmth, belonging, and intellectual depth together

What BBB content is NOT
Not generic
Never "5 tips to learn English faster." Always rooted in a real BBB moment, session, book, or member story.
Not corporate
Elaina's voice is warm, direct, and real. No stiff brand-speak. Content should feel like a conversation, not a campaign.
Not shallow
BBB's audience is smart and ambitious. Content should challenge, provoke thought, and respect their intelligence.
